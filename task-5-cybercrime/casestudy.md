# Case Study: UPI/Online Payment Fraud
**Author:** Aditya Prakash (25MIP10104)

## Overview
UPI fraud involves social engineering tactics to trick users into authorizing debit transactions under the guise of receiving money.

## Step-by-Step Execution
1. **Initial Contact:** Scammer contacts victim via a marketplace or social media.
2. **The Request:** Scammer sends a "Collect Money" request instead of a payment.
3. **The Trick:** Scammer convinces the victim that entering their PIN is necessary to "verify" or "receive" the funds.
4. **Execution:** Funds are instantly moved from the victim's account to the scammer's wallet.

## Target Demographic
Young adults/students (like those at VIT Bhopal) and individuals unfamiliar with the technical "Send vs. Receive" logic of UPI.

## Consequences
- Immediate, often irreversible financial loss.
- Potential for linked account exploitation.
- Loss of trust in digital payment systems.
